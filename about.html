    Our Story

    Who We Are

    Founded in 2014, AGOS Technology Ltd began with a simple yet powerful vision: to become Nigeria's most trusted partner for comprehensive business solutions. What started as a focused real estate venture has evolved into a multi-faceted organization serving clients across three critical sectors.

    Based in Uvwie, Delta State, we've grown from a small team of passionate professionals to a robust organization capable of handling projects of any scale. Our expansion into construction and technology services was driven by our clients' needs and our commitment to being a one-stop solution provider.

    Today, AGOS Technology stands as a testament to what Nigerian enterprise can achieve—combining local knowledge with international standards, traditional values with innovative approaches, and individual expertise with collaborative teamwork.
